

# PT Aurum Italia Nusantara — Corporate Website

A premium, minimal, one-page corporate website for a precious metals and strategic minerals company. Conservative, institutional tone with luxury-but-understated aesthetics.

## Visual Identity
- **Colors**: Deep navy/black base (#0B0B0B), soft ivory (#F5F2EB), metallic gold accent (#C6A55A)
- **Typography**: Elegant serif headings (Playfair Display), clean sans-serif body (Inter)
- **Feel**: Private investment firm / commodity trading house — discreet and credible

## Sections (in exact order)

### 1. Hero Section
Full-width dark background with centered headline: *"Connecting Indonesia's Gold Potential with Global Capital"*. Thin elegant sticky navigation (HOME | ABOUT | OPERATIONS | INVESTORS | CONTACT) right-aligned. Subtle outline "Invest with us" CTA button.

### 2. About Us
Two-column layout — text block on left, abstract dark mineral visualization on right. Includes a Company Prospectus download card with PDF button.

### 3. Operations & Projects
Split layout — intro paragraph left, "Operational Focus Areas" box right with bullet points covering gold exploration, local partnerships, compliance models, and commercial pathways. Compliance disclaimer line at bottom.

### 4. Investor Relations
Centered section on light background with 4 icon-based principles in a row: Regulatory Compliance, Commercial Clarity, Risk-Aware Structuring, Long-Term Alignment. Thin-line minimal icons.

### 5. Investor Documents
Premium document download grid with 4 PDF documents (Prospectus Overview, Investment Summary, Project Brief, Strategic Materials Brief). Each with icon, title, description, and download button. "Request Full Investment Materials" CTA below with qualification note.

### 6. Contact
Two-column — company address and details on left, minimal contact form (Name, Email, Message, Submit Enquiry) on right.

### 7. Footer
Minimal institutional footer with company name, address, email, and legal compliance line.

## Interactions & Responsiveness
- Smooth scroll navigation between sections
- Subtle fade-in animations on scroll (no flashy effects)
- Gold hover states on interactive elements
- Fully mobile-responsive with stacked vertical layout
